About Sweet Speech

At Sweet Speech, Alicia Weaver, M.A., CCC-SLP, is dedicated to providing compassionate, individualized therapy for children aged 6-12. Specializing in language delays, phonological and articulation disorders, stuttering, selective mutism, and auditory processing, Alicia’s goal is to help each child achieve their full potential. Offering both in-home visits and tailored therapy sessions, Sweet Speech creates a supportive and friendly environment that fosters growth and communication. With a focus on private pay, Alicia invites you to reach out directly via the contact form for more information or to discuss your child’s needs.
