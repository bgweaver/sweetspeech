About Sweet Speech

At Sweet Speech, Alicia Weaver, M.A., CCC-SLP, is dedicated to providing compassionate, individualized therapy for children aged 5-12. Specializing in receptive and expressive language delays, phonological and articulation disorders, stuttering, social language, and selective mutism, Alicia’s goal is to help each child achieve their full potential. Offering in-home visits with tailored therapy sessions, Sweet Speech provides warm, personalized support that fosters growth and communication. Sweet Speech operates as a private-pay practice, allowing for personalized therapy without insurance limitations. Alicia invites you to reach out via the contact form to learn more or to discuss your child’s needs.
