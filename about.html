Meet Alicia Weaver

I am a dedicated Speech-Language Pathologist with a passion for helping children communicate confidently and effectively. I graduated from Ball State University with a Master’s Degree in Speech-Language Pathology in 2008 and have had the privilege of working in a variety of settings, including long-term care, rehabilitation, home health, acute care, and outpatient pediatrics. However, my heart lies in the school setting, where I’ve worked full-time as a speech therapist since 2009.

Throughout my career, I’ve had the opportunity to support children with a wide range of communication challenges, and I have developed a special interest in working with children who have selective mutism. I am committed to helping all children express themselves and reach their full potential, fostering an environment where they feel safe, understood, and empowered.

As a proud member of the American Speech-Language-Hearing Association (ASHA), I continue to grow professionally and stay up-to-date on the latest research and best practices in the field of speech therapy.

When I'm not working, I enjoy spending time with my wonderful husband and our three amazing children. I love sharing family moments, cheering my son on at the baseball diamond, sipping on chai tea lattes, reading, and indulging in delicious Mexican food with friends and loved ones.

Let’s work together to help your child communicate confidently.
